## About me

I'm a Senior Frontend Engineer and Technical Lead based in Pune, India, with 5+ years building production-grade enterprise SaaS products. I've architected systems serving **1M+ users**, led cross-functional Agile teams, and driven **40–70% performance gains** across complex React/Next.js applications.

My current edge is at the intersection of **frontend engineering and AI systems** — building stateful LLM agent orchestrators, RAG pipelines, and MCP integrations that actually ship to production. I don't just build UIs. I own systems end-to-end, from architecture to deployment.
